Deep Reflective Journal Prompts

Use these structured prompts to move beyond surface-level feelings and explore the core of your mental and emotional state. **(Mental & Emotional Wellbeing Focus)**

Morning Clarity

What is one emotion I need to acknowledge today, and what is one small, actionable step I can take to nurture it?

The Resilience Audit

Describe a recent challenge. What did I learn about my own **inner strength** while navigating it?

Boundary Check

Where did I overextend myself this week? What does 'Self-Respecting No' look like for me tomorrow?

Gratitude Expansion

List three things I am truly grateful for, and describe the sensory details of *why* they bring me joy.

Unsent Letter

Write a letter to a feeling (e.g., Anxiety, Excitement). What is it trying to teach me?

Social Connection Score

Who did I authentically connect with recently, and how did that connection contribute to my sense of belonging? **(Social Wellbeing Focus)**

Mindfulness & Grounding Techniques

These exercises can be used anytime to instantly anchor yourself to the present moment, reducing overwhelm and increasing focus.

Box Breathing (4x4x4x4)

Method: Inhale slowly for 4 seconds, Hold for 4 seconds, Exhale slowly for 4 seconds, Wait for 4 seconds. Repeat 5 times. This technique calms the nervous system by regulating the vagus nerve.

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Method: Name 5 things you **see**, 4 things you can **touch**, 3 things you can **hear**, 2 things you can **smell**, and 1 thing you can **taste**. This is a powerful tool to interrupt panic/anxiety.
