Instant Stress Busters: Emergency Protocols

These are your **emergency protocols**—quick, effective techniques to deploy when stress levels spike rapidly. They are designed to engage your senses and break the cycle of anxious thought.

The 90-Second Mood Changer

Immediately listen to an intentionally distracting 90-second snippet of music, white noise, or a podcast. This breaks the emotional cycle and prevents rumination.

Search Quick Audio

The Sensory Reset Challenge

Focus entirely on one strong, physical sensation: hold an ice cube for 60 seconds, or chew a piece of extremely strong mint gum. This diverts brain energy from emotional distress to physical sensation.

Try a Micro-Activity

Micro-Walk & Observe (3 Minutes)

Stand up, walk to the nearest window/door, and identify five unique things you see outside. This physical and mental shift can reset your focus before returning to your task.
