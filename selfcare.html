Quick Stress Relief Exercises (5-10 Minutes)

When you feel overwhelmed, use these short, **effective exercises** to regain control over your emotional state. **(Mental & Emotional Wellbeing Focus)**

Progressive Muscle Relaxation

Systematically tense and release muscle groups from head to toe. This physical process distracts the mind and relieves tension buildup.

Mindful Movement (Shake it Out)

Put on a quick, upbeat song and move or shake your body freely for 5 minutes. Release physical energy trapped by stress.

Cognitive Reappraisal

Identify the catastrophic thought (e.g., "I will fail"). Reframe it: "I am challenged, but I can take one small step now."
